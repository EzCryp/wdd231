Non-Profit Membership Level
Non-Profit Membership Level
Annual Fee: 100Php
Ideal for: Registered 501(c)(3) organizations, schools, and charities.

Benefits:
  • Listing in the online business directory
  • Access to member-only events and newsletters
  • Free posting on community calendar
  • Discounted rates for event sponsorships
  • Recognition as a non-profit member at events
Bronze Membership Level
Bronze Membership Level
Annual Fee: 300Php
Ideal for: Small businesses and start-ups looking to increase local visibility.

Benefits:
  • All Non-Profit benefits, plus:
  • Business profile with logo and link on Chamber website
  • Ribbon cutting ceremony for new business or anniversary
  • One complimentary event admission per quarter
  • Social media welcome post
  • Member window decal
Silver Membership Level
Silver Membership Level
Annual Fee: 500Php
Ideal for: Growing businesses wanting to expand their network and reach.

Benefits:
  • All Bronze benefits, plus:
  • Priority placement in online directory
  • Two complimentary event admissions per quarter
  • One e-newsletter spotlight per year
  • Discounted advertising in Chamber publications
  • Eligibility to serve on committees
Gold Membership Level
Gold Membership Level
Annual Fee: 1,000Php
Ideal for: Established businesses and leaders seeking maximum exposure and influence.

Benefits:
  • All Silver benefits, plus:
  • Premium directory listing with featured placement
  • Unlimited complimentary event admissions
  • One sponsored social media post per quarter
  • Invitation to exclusive Gold Member roundtable events
  • Logo placement on Chamber homepage and event banners
  • First right of refusal for event sponsorships
